Klute

"Wipe your feet on the way out." With a tag-line like this, Klute is perhaps the smallest and stickiest club in Durham. Once voted Europe's worst nightclub, Klute is a student favourite, busy on any night of the week. Sunday-Night-Klute is a popular phenomenon among many of Durham's colleges. Think cheap drinks, fancy-dress and sticky floors.

Take me to Klute!

Ebony Champagne and Cocktail Bar

Perhaps a classier night out for Durham standards, Durham's premier Champagne and Cocktail Bar is located in the heart of the city. Ebony offers a range of vintage and non-vintage champagnes, over 40 different cocktails chosen by expoert mixologists, and fine wines, spirits and beers.

Take me to Ebony!

Jimmy Allen's

Situated under Elvet Bridge, the affectionatley named "Jimmy's" is a popular venue for students on both bar crawls, and also a popular destination for locals on the weekend. Free entry everynight, and cavernous rooms under the bridge make Jimmy Allens a Durham-favourite. Be sure to try their signature drink, the Jonny Woodgate if you are planning a visit.

Take me to Jimmy's!

If you like burgers this place is for you!

Tango is the place where burgers are grilled and stacked high with various toppings. From burgers being topped with many ingredients to fries being stacked with just as many ingredients too! Whether you go halves on a burger with your friend or intend on tackling one all by yourself you will not be disappointed!

Take me to Tango!

Flat White

Pancake heaven is where Flat White is at! From half stacks to full stacks topped with bacon or fruit- the options are endless! Or if you prefer your eggs sunny side up, poached with salmon or with a cream cheese bagel Flat White gives all it can offer and more! Flat white is the place to be for all your coffee cravings and hot chocolates topped with cream and marshmallows for kids! The cake selection will have both the adults and kids in an amazement

Take me to Flat White

La Spaghettata

La Spaghettata brings Italy to Durham! Expect stringy cheese pizza's made from a family inherited recipe, as well as creamy carbonara whhich will satisfy any taste buds. Lest not forget the 2 litre bottle of wine that can be purchased when dining in big groups! The perfect excuse to take the whole family!

Take me to La Spaghettata

The Cellar Door

The Cellar Door serves some of Durham's finest food. The ingredients are sourced locally, ensuring the best quality produce. The restaurant hosts a gourmet menu which changes frequently according to the seasonal ingredients available. The popularity of this restaurant is high so be sure to book early in advance!

Take me to The Cellar Door
